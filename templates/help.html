Booking
How do I book a service?

Simply go to our Booking page, fill in your name, phone, email, select the service you need, and submit. We will contact you within 2-4 hours to confirm your booking.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes! You can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Contact us via phone at +91 73392 78064 or email pranavcj2008@gmail.com.

How soon will I get a confirmation?

We confirm all bookings within 2-4 hours during working hours (Mon-Sat 9AM to 8PM). You will receive a WhatsApp or phone call from our team.

Can I book multiple services at once?

Absolutely! You can book multiple services. Just mention all the services you need in the message/requirements field when booking, or contact us directly.

Payment
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash. Payment details will be shared after booking confirmation.

Do I need to pay in advance?

We require a 50% advance payment to confirm your booking. The remaining 50% is due upon delivery of the final work.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges at all! The price shown on our services page is the final price. We believe in 100% transparent pricing.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes! We offer special discounts for bulk orders and long-term clients. Contact us directly to discuss custom pricing for your needs.

Delivery
How will I receive my final files?

We deliver all final files via Google Drive, WhatsApp, or email — whichever you prefer. High-resolution files are always included.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery time depends on the service: Logo Design (2-3 days), Video Editing (3-5 days), Website Design (7-14 days), Photography (1-2 days after session). Rush delivery is available on request.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all standard formats: PNG, JPG, PDF, AI, PSD for design; MP4, MOV for video; and all source files are included on request.

Revisions
How many revisions do I get?

We offer unlimited revisions until you are 100% satisfied with the final result. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

If you're not satisfied, we will redo the work completely at no extra cost. We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our services.

Can I request changes after final delivery?

Yes, minor changes can be requested within 7 days of final delivery for free. Major changes after that may be charged at a small fee.
